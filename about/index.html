About

I’m Bruno Brant, a Brazilian software engineer and architect with 15+ years of practice developing complex systems. I’m based in São Paulo, Brazil’s major capital. I love to work on algorithms and optimization, language development, general software architecture, and 3D development. When I’m not developing software, I’m usually playing games or reading.

This blog is a way to share some of my thoughts and to do some brainstorming. After all, we better organize our minds when we write about things.

If you like my posts, please subscribe to my feed.
